URG-T Dataset

Unsupervised Region Growing algorithm in Turbulence

Notice: This page is currently under development and will be updated with more information and resources.

The URG-T dataset consists of 20 videos recorded in outdoor environments at long-range with corresponding ground truth masks for motion segmentation. Scenes include urban outdoor scenes with moving vehicles, airplanes, and pedestrians, featuring up to 56 frames at 1920×1080 resolution. Captured using a Nikon Coolpix P1000 at effectively 3000mm due to sensor crop.

To address the lack of scenes with static backgrounds in the original dataset, additional static videos capturing various levels of atmospheric turbulence were added. These were filmed in desert environments under summer conditions, with subjects placed from 100 meters to 1 kilometer away, at 1080p resolution using a Nikon Coolpix P1000 with 125× zoom.
